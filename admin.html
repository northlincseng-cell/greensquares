retailer ↔ brand relationships

MGS must approve each brand campaign at each retailer before GS can be issued at that retailer's EPOS. This ensures all dual-award transactions are transparent, validated and CPRD-compliant.

green square equivalence

Each Green Square holds a standardised equivalence value — calculable, comparable, and independently validated by MGS. This value underpins every campaign at every tier. The minimum carbon standard is Verra VCS. No lower-grade credits are accepted. Net Zero and offset contributions must sum to 100%.
