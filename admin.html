green square equivalence

Each Green Square holds a standardised equivalence value — a calculable, comparable measure of carbon offset and fossil fuel reduction. This value underpins every campaign and every consumer message. The minimum standard is Verra VCS; no lower-grade credits are accepted. Net Zero and offset contributions must sum to 100%.
